CONSUMER CREDIT POLICY (synthetic, for demo/eval only)

Debt-to-income: An applicant is eligible for approval only if their total debt-to-income
(DTI) ratio does not exceed 43%. Applicants between 43% and 50% may be referred for manual
review with documented compensating factors such as significant cash reserves.

Income verification: Salaried applicants must provide the two most recent pay stubs and a
W-2. Self-employed applicants must provide two years of tax returns and a year-to-date
profit-and-loss statement.

Credit score: A minimum FICO score of 620 is required for standard products. Scores between
580 and 619 are eligible only for secured products with a 20% down payment.

Reapplication: An applicant who is denied must wait a cooling-off period of 90 days before
reapplying, unless the denial was due to a correctable documentation error.
