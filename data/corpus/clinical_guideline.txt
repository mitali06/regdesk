CLINICAL GUIDELINE: TYPE 2 DIABETES SCREENING (synthetic, for demo/eval only)

Screening eligibility: Screen asymptomatic adults aged 35 to 70 who are overweight or obese
(BMI 25 or higher). Screen earlier for patients with a first-degree relative with diabetes
or a history of gestational diabetes.

Diagnostic thresholds: A fasting plasma glucose of 126 mg/dL or higher, or an HbA1c of 6.5%
or higher, on two separate occasions confirms a diagnosis of type 2 diabetes.

Rescreening: Patients with normal results should be rescreened at least every 3 years.
Patients with prediabetes (HbA1c 5.7% to 6.4%) should be rescreened annually.

Referral: Refer to endocrinology if HbA1c remains above 9% despite two oral agents.
