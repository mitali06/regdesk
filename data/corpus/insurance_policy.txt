COMMERCIAL PROPERTY INSURANCE POLICY (synthetic, for demo/eval only)

Coverage: This policy covers direct physical loss to covered property caused by fire,
windstorm, hail, and water damage, subject to the deductible. Flood and earthquake are
excluded unless a separate endorsement is purchased.

Deductible: The standard per-occurrence deductible is $5,000. For named-storm events the
deductible is 2% of the insured value of the affected location.

Claims: Notice of a claim must be given within 60 days of the loss. The insured must
provide a signed proof of loss within 90 days and must take reasonable steps to protect
property from further damage.

Cancellation: The insurer may cancel for non-payment with 10 days written notice, or for
any other reason with 30 days written notice.
